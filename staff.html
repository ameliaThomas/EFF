The Eagle Field Foundation

The Eagle Field Foundation non-profit was founded in 2014 by a group of individuals driven to produce a new type of museum experience. Offering a unique exposure to hands-on-history with the goal of total immersion to stimulate educational knowledge and to keep history alive.
