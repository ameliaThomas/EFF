The Eagle Field Foundation

The Eagle Field Foundation non-profit was founded in 2014 by a group of individuals driven to produce a new type of museum experience. Offering a unique exposure to hands-on-history with the goal of total immersion to stimulate educational knowledge and to keep history alive.

Gatsby Summer Afternoon

Join several hundred aficionados of the 1920's and 30's as they step back to a time when elegance was a way of life. At the Gatsby Summer Afternoon, the panoramic front lawn of the historic Dunsmuir Hellman House becomes the stage on which every participant dresses in vintage day wear styles of the era. They play, picnic, sip champagne, and dance; the Charleston and Fox Trot is all the rage. It is reminiscent of a scene from the famous 1925 novel - The Great Gatsby by F. Scott Fitzgerald.
